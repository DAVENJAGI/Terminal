Welcome to Terminal, a world where we crunch for you your GitHub data bit by bit. Whether you need the analysis, visualization, Terminal is here to help you with that. Click "Enter Your Github Username" button to unlock the endless possibilities Terminal has got for you. Join us and lets dive into the world of your GitHub data together.

Imagine a sleek information box housing your profile details. This box prominently displays your username, alongside the dates it was created and last updated. To the side, two numbers showcase your total and public repositories, offering a quick glimpse into your coding activity.

But there's more! Click the dropdown arrow, and voila! A hidden section expands, revealing all the information again, presented in a details for closer inspection. This intuitive design gives you both a concise overview and the option to delve deeper, making it a versatile tool for you.
